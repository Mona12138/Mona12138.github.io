离散数学

Created2025-04-11|Updated2025-04-28
|Post Views:

引言、命题逻辑

内容

Propositions are statements that are true or false.

Propositional forms use ∧, ∨, ¬.

Propositional forms correspond to truth tables.

Logical equivalence of forms means same truth tables.

Implication: P =⇒ Q ⇐⇒ ¬P ∨ Q.

Contrapositive: ¬Q =⇒ ¬P

Converse: Q =⇒ P

P =⇒ Q ⇐⇒ ¬P ∨ Q.

Predicates: Statements with “free” variables.

Quantifiers: ∀x P(x), ∃y Q(y)

Now can state theorems! And disprove false ones!

DeMorgans Laws: “Flip and Distribute negation”

¬(P ∨ Q) ⇐⇒ (¬P ∧ ¬Q)

¬∀x P(x) ⇐⇒ ∃x ¬P(x).

练习

In parts (a)–(b), convert the English sentences into propositional logic. In parts (c)–(d), convert the propositions into English. For parts (b) and (d), use the notation a | b to denote the statement “a divides b”, and use the notation P(x) to denote the statement “x is a prime number”.

  1. For every real number k, there is a unique real solution to x3 = k.

A:(∀k ∈ R)[(∃x ∈ R)(x3 = k) ∧ (∀y, z ∈ R)(((y3 = k) ∧ (z3 = k)) =⇒ (y = z))]. - 唯一的表示方法 (∀y, z ∈ R)(((y3 = k) ∧ (z3 = k)) =⇒ (y = z))

  1. If p is a prime number, then for any two natural numbers a and b, if p doesn’t divide a and p divides ab, then p divides b.

(∀p ∈ N)[(P(p)) =⇒ ((∀a, b ∈ N)[((p | ab) ∧ ¬(p | a)) =⇒ (p | b)])].

  1. (∀x, y ∈ R)[(xy = 0) =⇒ ((x = 0) ∨ (y = 0))]

A: If the product of two real numbers is 0, then one of them must be 0.

  1. ¬((∃y ∈ N)[(∀x ∈ N)[(x > y) =⇒ ((y | x) ∨ P(x))]])
  • ¬(存在y为正整数，任意x为正整数，如果x>y,那么y可以整除x或者y是质数)
  • ¬(换句话说，这个 y 有一个特殊性质：它“能整除所有比它大的合数”（因为如果不能整除，就必须是质数）)
  • 不存在一个自然数 y，可以整除所有比它大的合数。 A:There is no natural number that divides every composite number greater than it.
  • 关键在合数（composite number），合数指的是除了1和其本身外具有其他正因数的正整数。
  • 所以展开来说就是不存在一个自然数y，可以整除所有比它大的合数。
